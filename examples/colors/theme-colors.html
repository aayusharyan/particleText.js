üí° Creating Your Own Theme:

Tips for choosing theme colors:

  • Use 3-4 colors from the same color family for cohesion
  • Include variations in brightness (light and dark shades)
  • Test colors on both light and dark backgrounds
  • Use online tools like Adobe Color or Coolors.co for palette creation
  • Consider color psychology and your brand identity
  • Ensure sufficient contrast for visibility
